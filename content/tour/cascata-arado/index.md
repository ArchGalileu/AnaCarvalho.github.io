## Experience the Arado Waterfall

Join us on an enchanting journey to the breathtaking Arado Waterfall, one of the most stunning natural gems in Peneda Gerês National Park. During this guided walk, you'll experience the majestic cascade up close, learn about the local ecosystem, and enjoy the pristine natural surroundings.

This tour offers an exceptional opportunity to appreciate the natural heritage of Portugal's only National Park, with your expert guide sharing fascinating stories about the region's geology, wildlife, and traditions.

## Tour Itinerary

- **08:30** - Meeting point near the waterfall area
- **08:45** - Safety briefing and equipment check
- **09:00** - Guided walk to the waterfall
- **11:00** - Swimming and relaxation time (weather permitting)
- **12:00** - Picnic lunch break
- **13:30** - Return journey and tour conclusion

## What to Bring

- Swimsuit and towel (for summer visits)
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Sunscreen and hat
- Water and light snacks
- Camera to capture the breathtaking views
